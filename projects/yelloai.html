YelloAI - AI-Powered Directory Service

A sophisticated directory and search platform that combines traditional business directory functionality with AI-powered recommendations and intelligent search capabilities. Built with a database-centric architecture utilizing PostgreSQL's advanced features including PLpgSQL stored procedures for complex business logic. The platform leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized recommendations, semantic search, and intelligent categorization of business listings and services.

Platform Overview

YelloAI reimagines the traditional business directory by incorporating artificial intelligence to provide smarter, more relevant results for users searching for businesses, services, and professionals. The platform uses advanced database techniques and AI algorithms to understand user intent and deliver personalized recommendations.

Core Architecture

Database-Centric Design

Built around PostgreSQL with sophisticated database architecture featuring:

  • PLpgSQL stored procedures for complex business logic
  • Advanced indexing strategies for performance optimization
  • Full-text search capabilities with custom ranking
  • Geospatial indexing for location-based searches
  • Materialized views for complex aggregations
  • Trigger-based data consistency and validation

AI-Powered Recommendation System

Intelligent recommendation engine that provides personalized business suggestions based on:

  • User search history and behavior patterns
  • Semantic analysis of business descriptions and services
  • Location-based relevance and proximity scoring
  • Review sentiment analysis and quality metrics
  • Category similarity and cross-recommendations
  • Time-based relevance (operating hours, seasonal services)

Intelligent Search Capabilities

Advanced search functionality combining multiple techniques:

  • Natural language query processing
  • Semantic understanding of search intent
  • Fuzzy matching for typos and variations
  • Auto-completion with intelligent suggestions
  • Multi-criteria filtering and sorting
  • Real-time search result optimization

Business Logic Layer

Comprehensive PLpgSQL implementation handling:
