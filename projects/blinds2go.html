Blinds2Go - E-commerce Platform Enhancement

A comprehensive enhancement of the Blinds2Go e-commerce platform specializing in custom window treatments and blinds. The project includes advanced product customization tools, AI-powered customer service integration, intelligent search and filtering capabilities, and sophisticated measurement and installation guidance systems. Built with modern e-commerce best practices, the platform provides a seamless customer experience from product discovery to installation support.

Platform Overview

The enhanced Blinds2Go platform transforms the traditional window treatment shopping experience by providing comprehensive customization options, professional guidance, and seamless ordering processes. The platform serves both residential and commercial customers with specialized tools for each market segment.
